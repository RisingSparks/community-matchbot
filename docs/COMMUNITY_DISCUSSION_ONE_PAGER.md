# Matchbot: Plain-Language Discussion Draft

## TL;DR
Matchbot is a **discovery tool**, not a recruitment machine.

It helps self-motivated people find camps, art projects, and infrastructure opportunities that fit their values and contribution style.

It also helps camps and art teams find aligned new energy, without forcing growth or bypassing community judgment.

## What This Is

- A lightweight helper that organizes already-public posts across Reddit, Discord, and Facebook.
- A way to make "looking for camp/project" and "we need builders" posts easier to find.
- A lightweight intro tool with human review when needed. Humans still decide fit.

## What This Is Not

- Not a "feed camps with warm bodies" system.
- Not an auto-placement or guaranteed acceptance tool.
- Not a ranking engine that tells people where they "should" go.
- Not a replacement for camp/art-team vetting, consent, or culture.

## Why We’re Doing It

Many people want to contribute, but struggle to find the right entry point.
Post-pandemic, three things got harder:

1. Affordability: shared infrastructure can lower barriers.
2. Acculturation: people still need places to learn participation norms.
3. Social access: friends-of-friends pipelines are weaker than they used to be.

Theme camps and art teams are still strong community nodes for mentorship and skill transfer.

## How It Works (Simple Version)

1. People post in existing community channels.
2. Matchbot detects relevant posts and extracts key details.
3. It proposes possible matches (seeker ↔ camp/project, or infra seeking ↔ offering).
4. A human reviews proposed intros before they are sent.
5. If approved, it sends a basic intro message so people can talk directly.

## Core Guardrails

- Discovery over recruitment.
- Contribution over consumption.
- Human judgment over automation.
- Community autonomy over centralized control.
- If a camp/project should be smaller, that is valid.

## Language We Use

Preferred:
- "Reducing friction for the interested."
- "Helping self-motivated people find their people."
- "Discovery tool, not a recruitment tool."

Avoid:
- "Recruiting funnel"
- "Feeding camps"
- Any promise of placement

## One-Sentence Version for Posts

"Matchbot is a discovery tool that helps self-motivated Burners find aligned camps, art projects, and infra opportunities; it does introductions, not recruiting or placement."
