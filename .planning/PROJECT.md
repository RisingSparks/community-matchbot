# Burning Man Matchbot

## Core Value
Reducing friction for the "interested builder" by indexing and organizing community posts (Reddit, Discord, etc.) to facilitate mentorship-driven matches between seekers and theme camps/art projects.

## Vision
Transform Burning Man participation from passive attendance to active building by strengthening the intergenerational continuity of theme camps as "natural classrooms."

## Constraints
- **Low Overhead**: Workflows must save time for TCOs/Leads, not add debt.
- **Participation-First**: Match for contribution, not consumption.
- **Passive-First**: Ingest from existing community channels before building net-new forms.

## Target Audience
- **Seekers**: Birgins and aspiring builders looking for a "way in."
- **Camps/Leads**: TCOs and Art Leads needing reliable hands and potential successors.
- **Ecosystem**: Sparks, Placement, and BMP teams seeking demographic continuity.

## Tech Stack
- Python 3.12+ (managed with `uv`)
- LLM-based structured extraction
- Platform listeners (Reddit/Discord APIs)
