# Privacy Notice: Burning Man Matchbot

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Notice explains how we handle your data. It should be read alongside our [Terms of Service](./TERMS.md).

## 1. About This Project
The **Burning Man Matchbot** (the "Bot") is an informal, volunteer-run community project. It is not a business, LLC, or official affiliate of the Burning Man Project. Our goal is to facilitate intergenerational mentorship and logistical sharing within the Burning Man community by matching "Seekers" with "Camps" and infrastructure resources.

## 2. Information We Collect
The Bot monitors specific public community channels on **Reddit**, **Discord**, and **Facebook**. We collect:
*   **Public Content:** The text of your public posts and comments related to camp-finding, mentorship, or infrastructure sharing.
*   **Identifiers:** Your platform username or display name, platform-specific User ID, and the URL of your post.
*   **Extracted Data:** We use automated tools to identify your intent (e.g., "seeking a camp" or "offering a mentor"), "vibes," and specific needs (e.g., "skills learning" or "infrastructure swap").

## 3. How We Use Your Information
*   **Matching:** To suggest potential connections between participants based on shared interests or logistical needs.
*   **Human Review:** Project volunteers may review extracted data and proposed matches to ensure quality and safety.
*   **Introductions:** If a match is approved, we may send you an automated message on the platform where you posted to facilitate an introduction.
*   **Feedback:** We may contact you to ask how a match went to improve the service.

## 4. Data Processing & Third Parties
To understand the intent and context of your posts, we use third-party Artificial Intelligence services:
*   **Anthropic (Claude)** or **OpenAI (GPT)**: Post text is sent to these providers for information extraction. This data is subject to their respective privacy policies regarding API usage (generally, they do not use API data to train their models, but we do not control their systems).
*   **Hosting:** Our database (SQLite) and application run on volunteer-maintained infrastructure.

## 5. Your Choices & Opt-Out
We respect your privacy and your right to not participate.
*   **To Opt Out:** You can stop the Bot from processing your future posts or sending you messages by sending a private message (PM/DM) with the exact text **"opt out"** to the Bot on the platform you are using (Reddit or Discord).
*   **Effect:** Once you opt out, your platform ID is added to a "Do Not Process" list, and we will no longer ingest your posts or propose you for matches.

## 6. Security & Retention
*   **Volunteer Effort:** We take reasonable steps to secure the data we collect, but as a volunteer project, we do not have the resources of a commercial service.
*   **Retention:** We retain data only as long as necessary for the current burn cycle and related community analysis.

## 7. Disclaimers
*   **No Guarantees:** This service is provided "as-is" without any warranties.
*   **Not a Business:** By using this service or interacting with the Bot, you acknowledge that this is a non-commercial, informal community effort.
*   **Public Data:** We only ingest data that you have already made public on community forums.

## 8. Contact
If you have questions or wish to request data removal, please reach out to the project maintainers via the platform where you encountered the Bot.
