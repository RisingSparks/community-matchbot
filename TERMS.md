# Matchbot: Terms of Service & Community Notice

**Last Updated: February 21, 2026**

Welcome to Matchbot! This is a volunteer-run, community project designed to help "Seekers" (newcomers and aspiring builders) find "Camps" (Theme Camps, Art Projects, and Infrastructure Leads) at Burning Man. 

By using this service or interacting with our bots on community platforms, you agree to this notice. Because this is a plain-language, informal project, we’ve kept this as simple as possible.

---

### 1. What Matchbot Does
Matchbot acts as a "connective tissue" for the Burning Man ecosystem. We use automated tools (bots) to scan public posts in approved community channels (like certain subreddits, Discord servers, or Facebook groups) to find people looking for camps and camps looking for members. 

Our goal is to reduce friction and help ensure the long-term continuity of Black Rock City by facilitating mentorship and participation.

### 2. How We Handle Your Data
*   **Public Information Only:** Our bots only "see" and index information you have already made public in community forums. We do not access private messages or non-public data.
*   **Summarization:** We use Large Language Models (LLMs) to summarize your public posts (e.g., extracting your "vibe," skills offered, or what you're looking for) so we can suggest potential matches.
*   **No Selling:** We do not sell your data. We only use it to facilitate introductions within the Burning Man community.
*   **Record Keeping:** We keep a record of these matches to help improve the service and report on whether the project is successfully helping the community (e.g., "100 people found camps this year").

### 3. Introductions, Not Endorsements
Matchbot provides **introductions**, not vetting. 
*   **Vetting is Your Job:** We do not perform background checks or "vouch" for any seeker or camp. Each party is responsible for their own vetting, safety, and intake process.
*   **No Guarantees:** We don't guarantee that you will find a match, or that a match will be a "good" fit. Burning Man is a high-stakes environment; please use your best judgment.

### 4. User Responsibilities
*   **Be Honest:** Don't provide false information about your skills, camp offerings, or identity.
*   **Be Respectful:** This is a community project built on the 10 Principles of Burning Man. Harassment or spamming via Matchbot-facilitated intros will result in being banned from the service.
*   **Ownership:** You represent that you have the right to post the information you share and that it doesn't violate anyone else's rights.

### 5. Opting Out & Data Deletion
If you don't want to be indexed by Matchbot, or if you want your data removed:
*   **Opt-Out:** You can tell the bot "opt out" (where available) or contact a moderator.
*   **Deletion:** We will delete your profile and match history upon request. Note that we cannot "un-send" introductions that have already happened.
*   **Platform Controls:** If you delete your original post on Reddit/Discord/Facebook, our bot will eventually mark its record as "stale" or "closed," though it may remain in our logs for a short period.

### 7. Changes to This Notice
As this project evolves, we may update this notice. The "Last Updated" date at the top will tell you when things last changed.

### 8. Contact
If you have questions, feedback, or want to request data deletion, please reach out to us via the community channels where you encountered the bot (e.g., mod-mail on the relevant subreddit or the Discord admin team).
